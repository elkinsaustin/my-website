About Us

Union Road Nature Project (URNP) is a grassroots group dedicated to transforming vacant land into welcoming natural spaces using native plants and inclusive, sustainable design. Our flagship project is a proposed pollinator prairie at 3635 Union Roadâ€”a long-vacant lot we aim to turn into a habitat for butterflies, birds, and beneficial insects.
